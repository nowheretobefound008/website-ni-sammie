Information and Communication Technology

The Information and Communication Technology (ICT) Strand is Part of the Technical-Vocational Livelihood (TVL) Track in the K-12 Curriculum. It Equips Students with Skills in Programming, Web Design, Animation, and Telecom Installation. Graduates Can Pursue Careers Such as Data Encoding, Programming, and Web Development. Choosing the ICT Strand Provides a Solid Foundation for IT-Related College Courses.

Humanities and Social Sciences

The Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) Strand is One of the Specialized Academic Tracks Available in Senior High School Curriculum. It Targets Students Who Have An Affinity for Subjects Related to Humanities, Social Sciences, Arts, Literature, and Culture.

Home Economics

Graduates of this Program are Equipped to Perform a Wide Variety of Administrative Tasks, Including Clerical Duties, Personal Management, Events Coordination, and Customer Service The Home Economics (HE) Strand is Part of the Technical-Vocational Livelihood (TVL) Track in Senior High School. It Focuses on Practical Skills and Livelihood Projects Related to Various Areas.

General Academic Strand

The General Academic Strand (GAS) in Senior High School Provide a Well-Rounded Education, Emphasizing Critical Thinking, Communication, and Academic Skills.

Accountancy, Business and Management

Often Referred to as the ABM Strand-Prepares Young Business Leaders for the Future. It Serves as a Foundation for Business-Related College Degrees and Covers Essential Concepts in Financial Management, Business Ethics, and Corporate Operations.
